Rules for patients

Novosibirsk Scientific Research Institute of Tuberculosis
Ministry of Health of the Russian Federation

These Rules have been developed in accordance with Clause 3 of Article 27 of the Federal Law of the Russian Federation dated 11/21/2011 No. 323-FZ "On the Basics of Public Health Protection in the Russian Federation", Federal Law of the Russian Federation dated 11/29/2010 No. 326-FZ "On Compulsory Medical Insurance of Citizens in the Russian Federation", Federal Law of the Russian Federation dated 02/23/2013 Federal Law No. 15-FZ "On the Protection of Citizens' Health from Exposure to Ambient Tobacco Smoke and the Consequences of Tobacco Consumption", the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, and other regulatory acts.

These Rules define the norms of behavior of patients and visitors of the Novosibirsk Scientific Research Institute of Tuberculosis of the Ministry of Health of the Russian Federation (hereinafter referred to as the Institute) when receiving medical services.

Following these rules is necessary in order to better meet the needs of citizens for medical care, as well as to ensure the safety of patients and staff of the medical institution.

Compliance with these Rules is mandatory for patients and visitors of the Institute (based on the Order of the Director of the Federal State Budgetary Institution "NNIIT" of the Ministry of Health of the Russian Federation No. 01-04/1-60 dated 08/12/2013)

Rules for contacting the emergency room (advisory bureau)

When making a routine visit to the emergency room of the Institute's clinic, the patient must:

·         Submit the following documents:identification document (passport); referral and medical documents (examination results), compulsory medical insurance policy; SNILS

·         To get acquainted with the rules of patient behavior in the Institute's clinic, read and sign an Informed voluntary consent for medical interventions.

·         Inform the doctor of complete and truthful information about your health, past illnesses, and treatment methods, given that deliberate distortion of information about your health may affect the correctness of the diagnosis, treatment, and affect the prognosis of recovery.

Правила пребывания в клинике института

·         while on treatment, it is necessary to follow all prescriptions and recommendations of the attending physician, immediately inform him about negative changes in his well-being during diagnosis and treatment;

·         do not self-medicate, including not using the methods of "traditional" medicine without consulting your doctor.;

·         adhere to the daily routine established in the department;

·         take care of the institute's property, keep its premises clean and quiet;

·         observe the sanitary and anti-epidemic regime: when entering the institute's clinic, put on shoe covers or change into a change of shoes, keep outerwear in the wardrobe; when leaving the ward, put on and wear a protective mask;

·         The patient can take walks on the territory of the institute with the permission of the attending physician or the head of the department.;

·         show respect and kindness in dealing with medical professionals and other patients; do not allow yourself to shout or use obscene language; do not take actions that could violate the rights of other patients and the staff of the institute.

Absolutely not:

·         пользоваться личной посудой;

·         use personal clothing during bacterial release;

·         visit other departments, areas of medical staff (resident, nursing, office of the head. department, senior nurse's office);

·         smoking inside the building and on the territory of the Institute;

·         stay on the territory and in the premises of the Institute in a state of alcoholic or narcotic intoxication;

·         to bring firearms, gas and cold weapons, poisonous, radioactive, chemical and explosive substances, alcoholic beverages and other objects and means into the institute, the presence of which at the visitor or their use may pose a threat to the safety of others;

·         gambling on the premises and on the territory of the Institute;

·         to remove documents received for review from the institute without official permission; to withdraw the results of the examination from the medical record (the patient's medical record is the property of the medical institution and is stored in the institute's clinic).

Liability for violation of these Rules

1. For violation of the rules of stay at the Institute's clinic, the patient may be discharged with an appropriate mark in the disability certificate.

2. Obstruction of the process of providing medical care, disrespect for the staff of the Institute's clinic, other patients and visitors; violation of public order in buildings, office premises and on the territory of the Institute; causing moral and physical harm to the Institute's staff; causing damage to the business reputation of the Federal State Budgetary Institution "NNIIT" of the Ministry of Health of the Russian Federation, as well as material damage to its property entails liability, provided for by the current legislation of the Russian Federation.
