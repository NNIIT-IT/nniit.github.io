Epidemiological monitoring of tuberculosis in Siberia and the Far East:  

    monitoring of the tuberculosis epidemic situation in the Siberian and Far Eastern Federal Districts using classical and molecular epidemiological research methods;
    identification of epidemiological risk factors for the spread of primary resistant tuberculosis in specific territories;
    comprehensive assessment of significant factors affecting the tuberculosis situation in the Institute's curation area.

Development of new anti-tuberculosis management mechanisms in Siberia and the Far East:

    analysis of the situation, planning and organization of anti-tuberculosis measures at various levels of subordination - research institutes, health management bodies in the constituent entities of the Russian Federation, head territorial anti-tuberculosis institutions;
    creation of a management model for tuberculosis control processes.

Improvement of diagnostic and treatment methods:

    development of an algorithm for comprehensive examination and treatment of patients with multidrug-resistant pulmonary tuberculosis in combination with chronic hepatitis of various etiologies, COPD, bronchial tuberculosis;
    development of complex treatment options and pathogenetic justification of optimal chemotherapy regimens for MDR-tuberculosis patients with combined pathology (viral hepatitis, COPD, bronchial tuberculosis);
    development of methods for the diagnosis and treatment of tuberculosis of the genitourinary system, as well as developing complications;
    development of targeted therapy methods for tuberculosis of various localization.

Improvement of surgical methods of treatment of tuberculosis patients:

    optimization of surgical treatment methods for common destructive forms of drug-resistant tuberculosis using the entire arsenal of phthisiosurgical means and modern technologies (combined collapsosurgical, resection, organ-preserving and minimally invasive methods) developed at the Novosibirsk Tuberculosis Research Institute;
    improvement of methods of preoperative preparation and postoperative follow-up treatment of patients with multidrug-resistant tuberculosis;
    surgical treatment of patients with tuberculosis of the genitourinary system.

Diagnosis and differential diagnosis of tuberculosis:      

   
  • development of an algorithm for tuberculosis diagnosis by optimal combination of classical and molecular genetic methods for M. tuberculosis detection;
  •   pathomorphological diagnosis and differential diagnosis of tuberculosis of various localizations (lungs, organs of the genitourinary system, lymph nodes); evaluation of the effectiveness of various methods of treatment of experimental tuberculosis on models of mycobacterial inflammation;
  •   optimization of medical and economic approaches to the diagnostic activities of tuberculosis institutions.


Features of the functioning of the central and peripheral nervous system in patients with multidrug-resistant pulmonary tuberculosis in standard TB therapy:

  
  •   electroencephalographic examination and evaluation of the main indicators of bioelectric activity of the brain of patients with pulmonary tuberculosis during treatment;
  •     quantitative sensory testing and evaluation of the function of fine fibers of the peripheral nervous system in patients with pulmonary tuberculosis on the background of anti-tuberculosis therapy;
  •     assessment
of the emotional parameters of MDR-TB patients before and during treatment.
